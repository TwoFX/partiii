If $U \subseteq X$ is an open subset and
\[\begin{tikzcd}
	0 \arrow[r] & \mathcal{F}' \arrow[r, "i"] & \mathcal{F} \arrow[r, "p"] & \mathcal{F}''
\end{tikzcd}\]
is exact, then
\[\begin{tikzcd}
	0 \arrow[r] & \Gamma(U, \mathcal{F}') \arrow[r, "i_U"] & \Gamma(U, \mathcal{F}) \arrow[r, "p_U"] & \Gamma(U, \mathcal{F}'')
\end{tikzcd}\]
is exact.
